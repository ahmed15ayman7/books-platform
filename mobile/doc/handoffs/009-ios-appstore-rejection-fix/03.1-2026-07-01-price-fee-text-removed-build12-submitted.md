## Reply Sent to Apple

All 4 of Apple's questions were answered in App Store Connect:

1. **Who uses paid content?** → No paid content or subscriptions exist. All features are free to all users.
2. **Where can users purchase?** → Nowhere. No IAP, no external purchase links, no subscription system.
3. **What previously purchased content?** → None. No purchase system exists.
4. **What paid content unlocked without IAP?** → None. No paid tiers, premium features, or locked content of any kind.

The reply also proactively disclosed the cart history (build 8 had a disabled $0.00 cart; removed from build 9 onward) and stated that a future version will introduce book purchases through Apple's IAP APIs at that time.
